# Contributing to HanDl-ng Discussions

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the HanDl-ng community discussions! This guide explains how to participate effectively.

## 🗣️ Starting a Discussion

1. **Search first** — Browse [existing discussions](https://github.com/HanDl-ng/Discussions/discussions) to check if your topic has already been covered.
2. **Choose a category** — Select the most appropriate category for your discussion:
   - **📣 Announcements** — Reserved for maintainers to share important updates.
   - **💡 Ideas** — Suggest new features or improvements.
   - **🙋 Q&A** — Ask questions about our projects.
   - **🗣️ General** — General conversation and community topics.
   - **🎉 Show and Tell** — Share your work or achievements related to our projects.
3. **Write a clear title** — A good title helps others find and understand your discussion quickly.
4. **Provide context** — Include relevant background, examples, or code snippets to help the community assist you.

## ✅ Best Practices

- **Be specific** — Vague questions get vague answers. The more detail you provide, the better help you'll receive.
- **One topic per discussion** — Avoid combining multiple unrelated questions in one post.
- **Use Markdown** — Format your discussion with Markdown for better readability. Use code blocks for code snippets.
- **Follow up** — If your question was answered, mark the best response as the answer to help others in the future.
- **Stay on topic** — Keep your replies relevant to the original discussion.

## 🤝 Responding to Others

- Be kind, patient, and constructive in your responses.
- If you know the answer to a question, please share it!
- Upvote helpful responses to surface the best answers.
- Avoid "+1" or "me too" comments; use reactions instead.

## 🚫 What Not to Do

- Do not post spam, advertisements, or off-topic content.
- Do not share personal information (yours or others').
- Do not post the same discussion multiple times.
- Do not use discussions to report security vulnerabilities — see our [Security Policy](SECURITY.md) instead.
- Do not use discussions to report bugs in our projects — use the Issues tracker in the relevant repository.

## 📋 Code of Conduct

All participants are expected to follow our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Violations may result in your discussion being edited, hidden, or removed, and your account being restricted from participating.

## 🔒 Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability in any HanDl-ng project, please **do not** post it publicly in discussions. Instead, follow the instructions in our [Security Policy](SECURITY.md).

## ❓ Questions About Contributing to Code

If you want to contribute code to one of our projects, please refer to the `CONTRIBUTING.md` file in the specific project repository.
